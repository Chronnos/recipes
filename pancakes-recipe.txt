Ingredients:
 - 750ml of water
 - 1 egg
 - 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
 - 4 coups of flour
 - Salt and oregano to taste

Directions:
 1. Mix every ingredient using a blender or mixer.
 2. Add flour until the mixture becomes thick.
 3. Fry a shell of dough in a pan using soy or olive oil, spreading it all over
 the pan's surface.
 4. Rising and drying the pancake's edge, turn it over to fry the other side.

Serves 20 portions.
